Administration

Dr. Alicia K. Scelso
Dr. Scelso began her career in 2000 as a Health and Physical Education teacher, coach, and activity advisor at Oakcrest High School. In 2009, Dr. Scelso became an assistant principal in the Egg Harbor Township School District at Fernwood Avenue Middle School, and in 2012 she was transferred to the Egg Harbor Township High School. Dr. Scelso then served as principal of Pequannock Township High School from 2016-2019. Dr. Scelso became the principal of Freehold Township High School on July 1, 2019. Dr. Scelso earned her Bachelor of Science degree from Salisbury University and her Masters and Doctorate degrees from Widener University.
Dr. Shae-Brie Dow
I am an educator with experience spanning over two decades. I have been an administrator for the Freehold Regional High School District for over 11 years. Prior to joining the FRHSD administrative team, I was a k-12 district science supervisor for a very large school district in northern NJ, and taught a range of classes from advanced placement to self-contained during my 7 years as a high school chemistry teacher. In addition to my experience in the field of public education, I have also served as an executive director with Dow Holdings, Incorporated; a Science & Mathematics Curriculum Development Supervisor with Ego-Ideal After School & Tutoring, Incorporated; and co-founded non-profit, Reaching Beyond Autism, serving as executive director as well. I hold a dual degree in Chemistry and Secondary Education, as well as advanced degrees in Educational Leadership, Management, and Policy.
